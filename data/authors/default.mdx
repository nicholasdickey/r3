Nicholas Dickey is a seasoned Software Developer and Engineering Leader.

His long career began in Boston's tech industry, evolved and matured in New York's financial services sector, and most recently, continued within the Silicon Valley tech scene.

Previously, Nicholas served as the Global Head of Technology and Development for Markit Counterparty Manager (also known as S&P Global Counterparty Manager) at Markit. He led an AEI consulting practice on Wall Street, published the first phone chess game (Quick Chess, 2002, on Verizon BREW), and more recently, headed the Growth Engineering team at Domino Data Lab.
